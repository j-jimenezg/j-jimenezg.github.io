About Me

I am a Puerto Rican student pursuing a bachelor's degree in Computational Mathematics. By participating in math competitions during middle school, I developed a passion for mathematics from a young age.

My studies in Computational Mathematics consist of taking courses related to Pure Mathematics and Computer Science, as well as Chemistry, Biology and Physics. Since my freshman year, I've used the skills adquired in my classes in undergraduate research, with projects related to computational chemistry and engineering.

In graduate school, I plan to specialize in Applied Mathematics, to keep discovering the many ways in which mathematics and computer programming can solve problems in fields inside and outside of the Natural Sciences.
